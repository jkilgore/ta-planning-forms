How to Submit a Sermon Series

A quick guide for pastors and teachers — from series idea to submission in minutes.

The Sermon Series form captures everything the team needs to plan, design, and support an upcoming series — series details, weekly message breakdowns, speakers, and scriptures. Fill it out as early as possible so creative and operations have enough lead time.

Step-by-step

1

Fill in the Series Details

Start with the big picture — what is this series called, what is it about, and when does it start?

Series name ✱ Start Sunday ✱ Number of weeks ✱ Series description Ministry goal Series scripture

The Ministry goal field helps align the series to one of our three tracks: Knowing God, Spiritual Practices & Worldview, or Discipleship Mindset.

2

Review the auto-generated weekly cards

Once you enter a start date and number of weeks, a card appears for each Sunday — pre-labeled with the correct date. Fill in what you know for each week; it's fine to leave fields blank if details aren't finalised yet.

Message title Message description Speaker Scripture reference Takeaway / challenge
3

Submit

Click Submit series. You'll see a green confirmation message when it goes through.

Helpful tips

  • Fields marked ✱ are required — you won't be able to submit without them.
  • You can change the number of weeks at any time and the cards will update without losing what you've already typed.
  • The Speaker field defaults to a placeholder — make sure to fill it in, even if it's TBD.
  • Submit early and update later — it's better to get a draft in than to wait until everything is perfect.
  • If the submission fails, check that you're connected to the internet and try again. Contact the operations team if it keeps failing.
